Our Mission

Here at TLJessop INC. we strive to be the best fictional space cat lodging service in the Milky Way. Your space will love their time here, in fact, you might have to drag them back onto your ship. Our staff has over 20 years of imaginary experience with space cat care and grooming, with each staff member living on-site year-round they have given their lives to the care of your space cats. There are eight large simulated gravity play areas each toned to be compatible with the sea level gravity of a Milky Way plant. We also special smaller play areas that can be custom toned the gravity and atmospheric conditions needed by your space cat upon request.
